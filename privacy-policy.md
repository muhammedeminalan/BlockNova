# Privacy Policy

**Nova Block** is developed by Muhammed Emin Alan.

This Privacy Policy explains how the Nova Block mobile application handles user information.

## Data Collection

Nova Block does not collect, store, or share any personal data. The app does not require account registration or login.

## Game Center

Nova Block uses Apple Game Center for leaderboards. Game Center is managed entirely by Apple. We do not access or store any Game Center data. Please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy for more information.

## Local Storage

The app stores your high score and game progress locally on your device using UserDefaults. This data never leaves your device.

## Third-Party Services

Nova Block does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.
